About Me

The Past

Having grown up in sunny Arizona for most of my life, I decided to move to the east coast in the spring of 2012. I was working in sales and hospitality for a few years before I got into coding. Coding very quickly became a huge part of my life and I soon noticed that I had a knack for building things and solving problems.

After researching bootcamps, online training programs, and even four year degree programs, I settled on Rutgers Coding Bootcamp. Being a career changer I knew I had to work harder than everyone else. It was the most difficult six months of my life but all that hard work paid off.

What I'm Up To Now

Before bootcamp was complete, I was one of three students recommended to go through the interview process to become an assistant instructor. It wasn't easy, but I became a T/A for another cohort, working part time teaching others.

I am currently working on React projects at an enterprise level with Yash Technologies contracting for John Deere. I can work with front-end/back-end/database technologies. I am willing to relocate just about anywhere and I am open to contract work.
